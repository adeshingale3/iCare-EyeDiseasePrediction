Retina Care

Advanced diagnostics and treatment for retinal diseases to prevent vision loss.

Our retina care department offers specialized care for conditions such as diabetic retinopathy and macular degeneration. Using AI-driven analysis, we ensure early detection and effective treatment to protect your vision.

Glaucoma Treatment

Early detection and management of glaucoma to preserve your vision.

Our glaucoma specialists utilize cutting-edge technology to monitor intraocular pressure and assess optic nerve health. Our AI-powered screening tools assist in detecting glaucoma at its earliest stages for timely intervention.

Cataract Surgery

Safe and advanced cataract removal procedures for clear vision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about eye care, disease prediction, and our services.

How does the Eye Disease Prediction System work?

Our system uses advanced machine learning models to analyze retinal images and patient-provided symptoms. Based on the analysis, it predicts the likelihood of various eye diseases such as glaucoma, cataract, diabetic retinopathy, and more.

What types of eye diseases can this system detect?

The system can detect and predict a range of eye diseases, including but not limited to glaucoma, cataracts, diabetic retinopathy, age-related macular degeneration (AMD), and other retinal disorders.

Is the diagnosis from the system 100% accurate?

While our system provides highly accurate predictions using state-of-the-art models, we always recommend consulting with a certified ophthalmologist for a comprehensive evaluation and diagnosis.

How should I upload my retinal images for analysis?

You can upload high-quality retinal images through our secure portal. Ensure that the image is clear and well-focused to receive the most accurate results.

Is the Eye Disease Prediction System safe to use?

Yes, our system follows strict data privacy protocols to ensure that your medical data is safe and secure. We use encrypted servers to protect all uploaded images and user information.

How long does it take to get the prediction results?

The system processes the uploaded images and symptoms within a few minutes, providing quick and reliable results. In most cases, predictions are available within 5–10 minutes.
